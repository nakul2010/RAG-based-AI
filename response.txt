To locate Creative Commons licensed images on the internet, there are a few ways to do so.

Firstly, you can use search engines like Google Images or Bing Images and filter your results by using the "License" option. This will show you which images have been licensed under Creative Commons.

Alternatively, you can also visit the Creative Commons website at search.creativecommons.org and search for images that are licensed in a way that's suitable for your needs. They also have a large collection of public domain resources that you can access.
